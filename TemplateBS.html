FIND OUT WHICH CAREER IS BEST SUITED FOR YOU:



In early 2015, when it was time for me to choose a career path, I realized that the pressure of deciding on a career path is widespread. Unfortunately, hundreds of thousands of students in rural India are not blessed with the same resources of career counselors and post-secondary school guidance. I decided to take action, by first visiting a number of schools in and around various rural and semi-urban parts of India; interviewing the school faculty and students regarding the problems that they face when it comes to further education and careers and potential solutions that could solve them. I decided that creating a technological platform on which these students could get information about the limitless possibilities would be the best solution.

MY MISSION

The millions of children who attend government-funded schools usually come from families that are not educated, and who work in unskilled roles. To effect upward social mobility in India, it is essential these young people receive career and educational guidance from outside their homes.
